About

Alexander Mattingley-Scott is a German artist whose work explores the relationship between landscape, atmosphere, memory, and spirituality.

Working across painting and mixed media, the work creates spaces that move between abstraction and representation. Drawing inspiration from nature, dreams, and inner experience, the paintings invite viewers into environments that feel both unfamiliar and strangely comforting. Rather than depicting specific places, the work seeks to evoke a sense of contemplation, mystery, and connection.
